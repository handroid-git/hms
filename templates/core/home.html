Why this system works

Structured around real hospital workflows and staff responsibilities.

Waiting Room Automation

Patients are queued professionally with first-come-first-serve logic, while still supporting appointment and emergency prioritization.

Patient History

Every patient keeps a growing medical history including vitals, diagnosis, medication, and record stamps.

Role-Based Access

Each staff category operates from its own dashboard with the tools needed for their exact workflow.

Billing Integration

Consultation, lab requests, medication, and payments can be tied into one controlled billing process.

Operational Visibility

Staff can monitor queue pressure, patient flow, and workload through practical dashboards.

Scalable Foundation

Built in Django with a structure that can grow into advanced notifications, chat, reports, and payroll.
